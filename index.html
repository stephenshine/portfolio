About Me

A creative, thoughtful developer based in London

I’m a back end web developer specialising in Java. I see myself as unique because I’ve got a creative background, but I’ve also got seven years experience working in large businesses. This means that I’m very customer focussed and always aim to make a product that works for the end user.

As well as being able to think creatively, I also have a very logical mind and get great pleasure out of solving problems. I believe that in a time where technology plays a key role in everyday life and there is so much choice out there, simplicity and end-user focus is what makes a technology stand out.

At the moment I’m working full time for the Financial Ombudsman, helping develop their most important piece of software and I’m also studying the Development Professional Program with Just IT.

I have a real passion for technology and don’t just see it as a job. Which is why I invest so much of my personal time into learning and understanding more about computers.

Skills

I believe the key to a great web developer is not just knowing a programming language really well. It’s about understanding computers at their core and working well with others. I have experience of working with Java, C#, and JavaScript. And I also have a good understanding of HTML, CSS and PHP. I also pride myself in having strong core skill.

Problem solving

I believe that as well as having the ability to learn and understand technology, it’s important to have the desire and drive to explore new things. I have this drive and want to stay in touch with what’s happening in the world around me. This can be seen in my work history.

Communication

My work at the Financial Ombudsman has made me comfortable doing this.

Organisation
